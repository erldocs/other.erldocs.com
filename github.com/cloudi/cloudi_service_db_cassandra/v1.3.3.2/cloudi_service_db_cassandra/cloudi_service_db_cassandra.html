CloudI Cassandra Data Module This ia a _very_ thin shim on top of erlang_cassandra from https://github.com/dieswaytoofast/erlang_cassandraNote that your results are "double ok"d. i.e., whatever erlang_cassandra sends back is sent back from send_sync. As a result, all successful responses will be of the form {ok, {ok, Response}} All failures from erlang_cassandra will be of the form {ok, {error, Reason}} send_sync errors will be standard 'cloudi' errors
