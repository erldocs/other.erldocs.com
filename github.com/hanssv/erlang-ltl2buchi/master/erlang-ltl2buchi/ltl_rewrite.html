ltl_rewrite

Module for rewriting of LTL formula.

Module for rewriting of LTL formula. The rewriting engine is very similar to what is outlined and used in Wring and the paper about Wring.

Functions

rewrite(Phi::ltl_formula()) -> ltl_formula()

Rewrite LTL formula. Pre-translation rewriting of LTL formula in the spirit of Wring. The rewrite rules used are listed below, the list of rules is heuristically found to reduce the size of the BÃ¼chi automaton resulting from translation of the LTL expression.
List of formulas (p, q, and r are arbitrary LTL formulas):

!ltrue ==> lfalse

!lfalse ==> ltrue

p & p ==> p

p & lfalse ==> lfalse

lfalse & p ==> lfalse

p & ltrue ==> p

ltrue & p ==> p

p & !p ==> lfalse

!p & p ==> lfalse

p | p ==> p

p | lfalse ==> p

lfalse | p ==> p

p | ltrue ==> ltrue

ltrue | p ==> ltrue

p | !p ==> ltrue

!p | p ==> ltrue

X p U X q ==> X (p U q)

p R q & p R r ==> p R (q & r)

p R r | q R r ==> (p | q) R r

X p & X q ==> X (p & q)

X ltrue ==> ltrue

p U lfalse ==> lfalse

[] (<> p) | [] (<> q) ==> [] (<> (p | q))

<> X p ==> X (<> p)

[] ([] (<> p)) ==> [] (<> p)

<> ([] (<> p)) ==> [] (<> p)

X ([] (<> p)) ==> [] (<> p)

<> (p & [] (<> q)) ==> <> p & [] (<> q)

[] (p | [] (<> q)) ==> [] p | [] (<> q)

X (p & [] (<> q)) ==> X p & [] (<> q)

X (p | [] (<> q)) ==> X p | [] (<> q)
