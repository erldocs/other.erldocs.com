ecron_time

Ecron counterparts of the built-in time functions.

Ecron counterparts of the built-in time functions.

This module wraps the standard time functions, erlang:localtime(), erlang:universaltime(), erlang:now() and os:timestamp().

The reason for this is to enable mocking to simulate time within ecron.

Functions

localtime() -> term()

universaltime() -> term()

now() -> term()

timestamp() -> term()
