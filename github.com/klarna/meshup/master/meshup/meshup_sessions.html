meshup_sessions

Session guarantees.

Session guarantees. Sessions provide the AD of ACID. Write-sets are written to durable storage either in their entirety or not at all. No attempt is made to isolate concurrent sessions from one another. We assume that the underlying storage substrate doesn't support atomic operations on more than a single key (and even if it did, since write-sets may contain data for different DBs, atomicity would require some additional thought).
