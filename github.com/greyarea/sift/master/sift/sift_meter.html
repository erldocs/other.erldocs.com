sift_meter

Meters measure the rate of events over a period of time.

Meters measure the rate of events over a period of time. Meters are created with a name and a time base, and they produce four metrics, NAME.avg, NAME.avg1, NAME.avg5, NAME.avg15. The first is the average rate of all events since the metric was created. The rest are exponentially weighted moving averages calculated similar to load averages in Linux.
