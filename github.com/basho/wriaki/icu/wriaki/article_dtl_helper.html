article_dtl_helper

Parameterized module wrapper for Article.

Parameterized module wrapper for Article. Allows ErlyDTL templates to access properties of article with dotted notation.

Article A riak_object from either the "article" or "archive" bucket.

V The article revision to choose (almost always *the* revision of Article, but useful when Article has multiple revisions through riak siblings).

Functions

key() -> binary()

get the key of the article

key_urldecoded() -> term()

path() -> iolist()

get the URL-path to the article

encoded_vclock() -> binary()

get the vclock for Article, base64-encoded

text() -> binary()

get the plain-text format of Article

html() -> string()

get the html format of Article note: this is an html fragment, not a whole HTML document

msg() -> binary()

get the commit message of Article

history() -> [history_props()]

get the history summary of Article

has_multiple() -> boolean()

true if Article contains multiple revisions (riak-siblings), false if Article contains just one revision

tip_versions() -> [version()]

list of revisions contained in Article

selected_version() -> version()

revision of Article chosen for display
