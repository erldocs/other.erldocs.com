riak_kv_mrc_sink

This FSM acts as a Riak Pipe sink, and dumbly accumulates messages received from the pipe, until it is asked to send them to its owner.

This FSM acts as a Riak Pipe sink, and dumbly accumulates messages received from the pipe, until it is asked to send them to its owner. The owner is whatever process started this FSM.

merge_outputs(Acc::[[{integer(), list()}]]) -> [{integer(), list()}]

Convenience: If outputs are collected as a list of orddicts, with the first being the most recently received, merge them into one orddict.

That is, for one keep, our input should look like: [ [{0, [G,H,I]}], [{0, [D,E,F]}], [{0, [A,B,C]}] ] And we want it to come out as: [{0, [A,B,C,D,E,F,G,H,I]}]
