clocksi_general_tx_coord_fsm

The coordinator for a given Clock SI general tx_id.

The coordinator for a given Clock SI general tx_id. It handles the state of the tx and executes the operations sequentially by sending each operation to the responsible clockSI_vnode of the involved key. when a tx is finalized (committed or aborted, the fsm also finishes.
