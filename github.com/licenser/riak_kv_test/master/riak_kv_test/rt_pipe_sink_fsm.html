rt_pipe_sink_fsm

Sink fsm for eunit tests.

Sink fsm for eunit tests. It accumulates all pipe results/logs until eoi, then delivers them to the caller of get_results/1.

There are two states: acc and wait

The FSM starts out in acc simply accumulating results and logs.

If a get_results arrives during acc, the FSM stores the From, and continues accumulating results/logs in acc.

If an eoi comes in during acc before get_results, the FSM transitions to wait, where it stops accumulating results/logs, and just waits for get_results.

As soon as both eoi and get_results have arrived, the results/logs are delivered to the caller of get_results/1, and the FSM exits.

The sink can be told not to ack a list of {FittingName, Output} pairs, for testing purposes, by passing such a list as the value of a skip_ack property in the Options parameter of start_link/2. For example, to tell the sink not to ack the output bar from the fitting foo:

rt_pipe_sink_fsm:start_link(PipeRef, [{skip_ack, [{foo,bar}]}])

The skipped output will still be added to the results list.
