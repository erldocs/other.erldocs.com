svg_diff(Sx, Sy, DestFileName) -> term()

Example: 

    svg_diff("A cat ate my hat", "A dog ate my shoe", "tdiff-trace.svg").

The resulting svg file works well in Chrome and Opera.

The Sx is (usually) the "old" string. The Sy is (usually) the "new" string. The blue number in the upper left is the D number. Taking a step to the right means deleting that character. Taking a step down means inserting that character.

The tdiff algorithm can diff any list of objects, such as lists of lines, lists of words or whatever. However, this tracer is only good at strings (lists of characters). It might produce funny or strange svg files in other cases.
