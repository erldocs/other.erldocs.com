A Hostile Non-Player Character (NPC).

A Hostile Non-Player Character (NPC). NPCs are the games way of interacting. NPCs are controlled by internal logic. NPCs can be spawned by rooms and will attack players who enter their room. NPCs are not your friends the do not want to talk they only want to kill you.
