Master Management This behaviour is nearly equivalent to a standar gen_server that registers locally under the 'Callback' atom, and it is intended to be started at several nodes at the cluster. One of them will be elected master, and the callback handle_master/2 will be called with its pid(), or 'undefined' if the vnode fails.
