normalize_encoding(Name::string() | atom()) -> string() | atom()

Normalize encoding name. All alphanumeric characters are lowercased. All non-alphanumeric characters are collapsed and replaced with a single underscore, e.g. ' -;#' becomes '_'. Leading and trailing underscores are removed.
