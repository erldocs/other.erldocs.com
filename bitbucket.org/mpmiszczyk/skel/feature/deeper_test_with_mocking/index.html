sk_farm_collector
This module contains the collector logic of a farm` skeleton. A farm skeleton has the most basic kind of stream parallelism - inputs are sent to one of `n` replicas of the inner skeleton for processing. The collector takes inputs off the inner skeletons output stream and sends them out on the farm skeleton's output stream.
